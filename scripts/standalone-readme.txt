Zahpy Business Pro - Standalone Edition

How to run:
1. Extract the zip file to a folder on your Windows device.
2. Double-click Zahpy Business Pro.exe from the extracted folder.

What this desktop app does:
- Create invoices and estimates.
- Save editable invoice and estimate history locally.
- Manage saved clients.
- Maintain a reusable product and service catalog.
- Record invoice payments and track paid, partial, and outstanding balances.
- Save business defaults, tax settings, currency settings, payment links, and due-date defaults.
- Export finished documents as PDFs.
- Generate email drafts and open supported external email services.
- View local dashboard totals and payment status summaries.
- Protect the local workspace with an optional PIN lock.

Local data and backups:
- Zahpy Business Pro stores app records on this device for the current Windows user profile.
- The standalone edition does not automatically upload, sync, host, or sell your invoices, clients, catalog items, payments, or settings through a Zahpy cloud service.
- Use Save to History before closing the app when you want an editable invoice or estimate record.
- Use the History tab's Backup button regularly to export saved invoices, clients, catalog items, payments, business settings, and tax settings.
- Restoring a backup replaces the current local workspace with the contents of the backup file.

Internet and external services:
- The core app can run locally and offline after extraction.
- Update checks, GitHub downloads, email compose buttons, PayPal links, Stripe links, and other external links require internet access and may open third-party websites or apps.
- Third-party services are governed by their own terms, fees, privacy policies, availability, and account requirements.

Professional responsibility:
- Zahpy Business Pro is a business document tool.
- It is not legal, tax, accounting, financial, or compliance advice.
- Users are responsible for checking document numbers, totals, tax rates, late fees, payment terms, business information, client details, and recordkeeping requirements before sending or relying on documents.

Security and updates:
- The optional PIN lock protects the local workspace on this device, but forgotten PINs cannot be recovered.
- Keep separate backups before updating, deleting files, restoring data, or moving to another computer.
- Windows may show a warning because this build is not code-signed yet.
