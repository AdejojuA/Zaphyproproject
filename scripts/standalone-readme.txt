Zahpy Business Pro

How to run:
1. Extract this zip file.
2. Double-click Zahpy Business Pro.exe from the extracted folder.

Included app tools:
- Client manager
- Product/service catalog
- Payment tracking
- Tax and business defaults
- Offline core app assets

Notes:
- Windows may show a warning because this app is not code-signed yet.
- The core app runs locally and offline. External links such as Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail, PayPal, and Stripe still require internet access.
- Saved records are stored on the user's device. Use the History tab's Backup button regularly.
- Zahpy Business Pro is a document tool, not legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice. Users should verify totals, tax rates, payment terms, and business details before sending documents.
