Terms & Data Notice

Important Notice: Zahpy Business Pro is a local desktop app. Your records are stored on your device, not on a Zahpy cloud server. Back up your workspace regularly. Device failure, app data removal, or clearing local storage can permanently remove saved records if you do not have a backup.

1. Local Data & Ownership

Invoices, estimates, clients, catalog items, payments, design settings, and business settings are saved locally on the device where the app is used. The app does not automatically upload, sync, or store those records on external Zahpy servers.

2. Backups & Recordkeeping

Because saved data is local, you are responsible for keeping business records and backups. Recommended workflow:

  • Export PDFs: Keep final PDF copies of documents you send to clients.
  • Save to History: Save editable invoices and estimates before leaving the app.
  • Backup Workspaces: Use the History tab's Backup button to download a file containing your saved invoices, clients, catalog items, payments, and settings.
  • Restore Carefully: Restoring a backup replaces the current local workspace with the backup data.

3. Accuracy & Professional Responsibility

Zahpy Business Pro helps prepare business documents, but it is not legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice. You are responsible for checking invoice totals, tax rates, late fees, payment terms, business information, and client details before sending a document.

4. External Services

Email buttons, PayPal links, Stripe links, and other external links open third-party services. Those services require internet access and are governed by their own terms, fees, privacy policies, and account requirements.

5. PIN Lock & Data Loss

The optional PIN lock protects the local workspace. Forgotten PINs cannot be recovered. Zahpy Business Pro and its developers are not responsible for lost records, incorrect business entries, failed backups, third-party service issues, or losses related to use of the software.
