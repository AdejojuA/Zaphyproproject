📜 Terms & Conditions

Important Notice: Zahpy is a completely offline application running inside your browser. We do not use cloud servers. Zahpy is not responsible for any loss of data. Clearing your browser cache will permanently erase your invoices if they are not backed up.

1. Offline-First Architecture & Data Ownership

Zahpy Pro operates entirely within your web browser. We do not transmit, sync, or store your invoices on any external cloud servers or databases. Your data belongs solely to you and is stored locally on your device.

2. How to Safeguard Your Data

Because your data is not backed up to a cloud server, you are responsible for maintaining your own records. To prevent data loss due to device failure, browser updates, or accidental cache clearing, we strongly advise the following workflow:

  • Export as PDF: Always download a final PDF copy of every document you create. Store these in a secure folder on your computer or a personal cloud drive.
  • Save to History: Click "Save to History" to keep an editable version in your browser's local storage for future reference.
  • Print Physical Copies: For critical financial records, print hard copies to maintain in your physical filing systems.
  • Backup Workspaces: Go to the History tab regularly and use the "Backup" button to download a complete file of your entire Zahpy database.

3. Limitation of Liability

Zahpy Business Pro and its developers accept no liability for financial loss, lost documents, or data corruption resulting from the use of this software, browser/OS updates, hardware failure, or accidental cache clearance.
